## fig1_optimism_gap — leakage demonstration
**Plots:** per-model CV R² under a random split vs a study-grouped split.
**Read:** tree models reach R²≈0.55 under random splits but collapse to R²<0 under study-grouped CV
(optimism gap 0.62–0.85). **So:** naive pooling overstates generalization; this is the methodological
control that motivates everything downstream. Foil: Das 2023 single-study in-sample ANN (R²≈0.998).

## fig2_variance_decomposition — HEADLINE
**Plots:** between-study study-mean-yield variance explained (R²). Left: physical regime
(`system_class`, 0.02) vs all method covariates jointly (0.55). Right: per-method-covariate breakdown.
**Read:** methodological covariates explain ~27× more between-study variance than the physical regime;
**temperature-control method alone explains 0.33.** **So:** the apparent contradictions are
predominantly methodological (associational; survives control for regime — `TB_confound_check.md`).

## fig3_particle_size_consistency — flagship contradiction resolved
**Plots:** within-study Spearman ρ (yield vs particle size) for each study that varies size.
**Read:** 5/5 studies negative (smaller particle → higher yield). **So:** the headline "direction-
changing" particle-size contradiction is internally consistent within studies — it is a cross-study
integration artifact, not a physical sign reversal.

## fig4_ea_spread — Eₐ does not organize by regime
**Plots:** per-study apparent Eₐ (points) + class median (red bar) per `system_class`; literature band
3.5–102.6 kJ/mol shaded.
**Read:** within-regime spread ≈ between-regime spread (liquid_metal spans 8.5–58 kJ/mol). **So:** the
coarse regime label does not capture the kinetics; finer kinetic↔diffusion physics (Saceleanu 2019)
operates within studies — hence "predominantly", not "purely", methodological.

## fig5_dataset_composition — data resource
**Plots:** per-`system_class` yield-row and study counts; quality-tier stack; kinetic table size.
**Read:** 315 yield rows / 31 studies (3 classes ≥40, 2 exploratory) + 76-row kinetic table.
**So:** a curated, provenance-tracked, QC'd open dataset (CC-BY) — a standalone contribution.

**Caveats (all figures):** associational, not causal; n=31 studies / 3–9 per regime → nulls are
"no detectable effect at this n", not "no effect"; thin exploratory classes (waste_al, liquid_metal).
