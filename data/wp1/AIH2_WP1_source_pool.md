## Notable contradiction evidence already surfaced

- **Direction-changing particle size (H1):** `jayaraman2015` (30 µm < 350 µm yield, inverted) and `martinezv2026` (inverted-U cumulative H₂ vs size, SCM R²≈0.999) directly stage the size-effect contradiction.
- **Activation-energy spread (H3):** reported Eₐ ranges across the pool span ~10–158 kJ/mol (`chen2020` 10.4 · `meng2023` 18.2 · `guan2019` 20.1 · `huang2023` 39.1 · `fadhilah2023` 47.4 · `testa2024` ~50 · `noland2020` 60–65 · `wen2018` 73–158), bracketing the 3.5–102.6 kJ/mol literature band.
- **Rate–yield trade-off (contradiction #3):** `davies2022mat` (Zn slows rate, preserves 99.5% yield) and `manilevich2020` (Bi lowers rate) give clean trade-off cases.
